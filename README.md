# MATH-6243-final-project
Here is the repository for all of our resources for the final project :)


We are planning to look at a kaggle dataset that combines data collected from these two original locations: https://opendata.minneapolismn.gov/datasets/police-stop-data/explore and https://opendata.minneapolismn.gov/datasets/police-use-of-force. Both look at police stops and violence that occured specifically in Minneapolis from 2016 to late 2024 for stops, and 2004 to late 2024 for violence.
